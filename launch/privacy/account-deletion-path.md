# Account Deletion Path Evidence

Status: Draft.

## Public Path

Account deletion requests are routed through https://rbdarts.app/account/delete.

## App Surface

- iOS: `PrivacyAndSupportView` includes Account Deletion.
- Android: `PrivacyAndSupportScreen` includes Account Deletion.

## Required Before Submission

- Public page explains identity verification.
- Public page confirms expected response window.
- Support owner receives and tracks deletion requests.
- Backend deletion process removes user-owned gameplay records according to policy.
