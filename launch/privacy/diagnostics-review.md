# Privacy-Safe Launch Diagnostics Contract

## Allowed Launch Signals

- App launch success/failure count.
- Sign-in success/failure category without provider token or email.
- Score-save success/failure category without player names or correction reasons.
- Recovery path success/failure.
- Backend availability and callable error category.
- Crash-free sessions and non-identifying crash grouping.

## Forbidden Data

- Access tokens, refresh tokens, ID tokens, authorization codes, private keys, or
  app-specific passwords.
- Reviewer credentials or beta tester contact details.
- Full email addresses unless explicitly required by the support channel.
- Unredacted correction reasons.
- Raw support messages in analytics or crash metadata.
