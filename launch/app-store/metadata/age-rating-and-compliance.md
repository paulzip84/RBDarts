# App Store Age Rating And Compliance Worksheet

Status: Draft.

## Age Rating Inputs

- Cartoon or fantasy violence: none.
- Realistic violence: none.
- Mature or suggestive themes: none.
- Horror or fear themes: none.
- Medical or treatment information: none.
- Alcohol, tobacco, or controlled substance references: none.
- Gambling or contests: none.
- User-generated content: no public posting or messaging in MVP scope.
- Unrestricted web access: only support, privacy policy, and account deletion links.

## Compliance Inputs

- Encryption: standard platform networking only unless final build adds custom cryptography.
- Sign in with Apple: review if Apple requires parity for third-party SSO at submission time.
- Kids category: no.
- App Tracking Transparency: not planned; no cross-app tracking behavior is in MVP scope.
