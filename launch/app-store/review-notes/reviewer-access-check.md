# Reviewer Access Validation

Status: Draft.

## Checks

- Reviewer can reach sign-in without special device configuration.
- Google and Facebook test identities are active before submission.
- Test identity has no personal production data.
- Test identity can create and complete a standalone game.
- Privacy, support, and account deletion links are reachable without sign-in.
- Any review-only instructions are entered in App Store Connect, not committed.
