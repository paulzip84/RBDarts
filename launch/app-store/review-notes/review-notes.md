# App Store Review Notes

Status: Draft.

## Reviewer Path

RBDarts uses Google and Facebook SSO. For review, provide a revocable test identity through App Store Connect review information or approve a no-production-data demo path before submission. Do not commit reviewer credentials to this repository.

## Core Flow To Review

1. Launch RBDarts.
2. Sign in with an approved SSO provider.
3. Create a standalone baseball darts game with two sample players.
4. Enter scores for multiple innings.
5. Complete the game and review the summary.
6. Open the Privacy screen from the app toolbar.
7. Open Support, Privacy Policy, and Account Deletion links.

## Notes

- No purchases, subscriptions, ads, gambling, or children-directed content are included in the MVP launch scope.
- Match data is used for gameplay, scoring recovery, and aggregate stats.
- Diagnostics are privacy-filtered before collection.
