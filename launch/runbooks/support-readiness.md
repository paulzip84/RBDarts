# Support Readiness Runbook

Status: Draft.

## Channels

- Public support URL: https://rbdarts.app/support
- Account deletion URL: https://rbdarts.app/account/delete
- Store review messages: App Store Connect and Play Console

## Required Before Launch

- Support owner assigned.
- Response templates prepared for sign-in, scoring recovery, account deletion, and store review questions.
- Triage tags defined for crash, sign-in, scoring, privacy, and account deletion.
- Escalation path confirmed with engineering owner.

## First Release Window

- Review support queue twice daily for first three public launch days.
- Escalate critical issues immediately.
- Record repeated issues in launch readiness follow-up notes.
