## Severity Rules

- Critical: app cannot be reviewed, sign-in blocked, privacy disclosure mismatch, crash during core flow.
- High: metadata or screenshot mismatch, reviewer cannot access required feature, content rating ambiguity.
- Medium: clarification request or non-blocking metadata correction.
- Low: cosmetic or documentation follow-up.

## Response Rule

Assign an owner and draft response within one business day.
