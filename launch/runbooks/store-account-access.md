# Store Account Access Checklist

## Required Access

- Apple Developer Program team membership.
- App Store Connect access for app records, TestFlight, App Review, app privacy, screenshots, pricing/availability, and release controls.
- Google Play Console access for app setup, testing tracks, production releases, App Content, Data Safety, store listings, and review communications.
- Firebase project access for Auth, Firestore, Functions, App Check, Crashlytics, and Performance.
- Identity provider console access for Google, Facebook, and Apple sign-in setup.
- DNS/content management access for privacy policy, support, and account deletion URLs.
