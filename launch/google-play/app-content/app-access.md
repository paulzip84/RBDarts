# Google Play App Access

Status: Draft.

## Reviewer Path

RBDarts uses Google and Facebook SSO. For Play review, configure app access instructions in Play Console with an approved test identity or a review-safe demo path. Do not commit reviewer credentials to this repository.

## Core Flow To Review

1. Launch RBDarts.
2. Sign in with an approved SSO provider.
3. Create a standalone baseball darts game with two sample players.
4. Enter scores for multiple innings.
5. Complete the game and review the summary.
6. Open Support, Privacy Policy, and Account Deletion links.

## Notes

- The app does not sell paid goods.
- The app does not include ads.
- The app is not positioned as gambling.
- Account deletion is available through a public support path.
